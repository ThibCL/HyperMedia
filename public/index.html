Agir pour l'environnement

The association

Agir pour l'Environnement is a national citizens' association for environmental protection. The aim of the association is to put pressure on political leaders and economic decision-makers by carrying out citizen mobilisation campaigns bringing together the widest possible network of associations and citizens.
